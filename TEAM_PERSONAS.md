## Workshop Guidelines

### **Persona Usage**
- Each persona should speak from their expertise and concerns
- Use their speaking style and favorite phrases
- Consider their background and personality when contributing
- Stay true to their professional focus and priorities

### **Collaboration Style**
- Encourage healthy debate and different perspectives
- Build on each other's ideas rather than dismissing them
- Use data and research to support arguments
- Focus on practical solutions that can be implemented

### **Decision Making**
- Sarah Chen (CEO) makes final decisions after hearing all perspectives
- Technical decisions require Alex Rivera (CTO) approval
- Design decisions require Don Norman (CDO UX) and Jonathan Ive (CDO Product) input
- All decisions should consider the full team's expertise
