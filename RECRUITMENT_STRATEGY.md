# Spradley Team Recruitment Strategy
*Attracting World-Class Talent to Transform an Industry*

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the comprehensive recruitment strategy for assembling the world-class team that will transform Spradley from an MVP into the industry-defining platform for AI-powered employee experience.

## Recruitment Philosophy

### **Quality Over Speed**
- We will only hire the absolute best candidates for each role
- Better to wait for the right person than hire the wrong person
- Each hire must be a "10x" contributor who can transform their function

### **Cultural Fit First**
- Every candidate must align with our transformation mission
- We seek leaders who are passionate about improving employee experience
- Cultural fit is non-negotiable, regardless of technical expertise

### **Diversity & Inclusion**
- We actively seek diverse candidates across all dimensions
- Different perspectives drive better product decisions
- Our team should reflect the global workforce we serve

## Compensation Strategy

### **Competitive Compensation**
- Base salaries at 90th percentile for each role
- Equity packages that align with company success
- Performance bonuses tied to specific metrics
- Long-term incentives for retention

### **Non-Financial Benefits**
- Opportunity to reshape an entire industry
- Work with world-class colleagues
- Flexible work arrangements
- Professional development and conference attendance
- Equity in a high-growth company

### **Unique Value Propositions**
- **Mission-Driven Work**: Transform how organizations understand and improve employee experience
- **Technical Excellence**: Work with cutting-edge AI and design technologies
- **Global Impact**: Build products that improve work for millions of people
- **Learning Opportunity**: Work alongside industry legends and thought leaders

## Recruitment Channels

### **Direct Outreach**
- Personal networks and referrals from existing team
- LinkedIn and professional networking
- Industry conference and event networking
- Thought leadership content and speaking

### **Executive Search Firms**
- Partner with top-tier executive search firms
- Focus on firms with enterprise software expertise
- Leverage their networks and relationships
- Use their expertise in complex negotiations

### **Industry Partnerships**
- Partner with design schools and universities
- Engage with AI research institutions
- Collaborate with enterprise software vendors
- Build relationships with industry associations

### **Content Marketing**
- Create compelling content about our vision
- Share thought leadership on employee experience
- Publish case studies and success stories
- Speak at industry conferences and events

## Candidate Assessment Process

### **Initial Screening**
- Resume and portfolio review
- Initial phone/video screening
- Cultural fit assessment
- Basic technical competency check

### **In-Depth Interviews**
- Multiple rounds with different team members
- Case studies and problem-solving exercises
- Cultural fit and mission alignment assessment
- Technical depth and expertise evaluation

### **Reference Checks**
- Speak with former colleagues and managers
- Verify achievements and impact
- Assess cultural fit and leadership style
- Confirm availability and interest

### **Final Decision**
- Team consensus on candidate fit
- Compensation and equity negotiation
- Start date and transition planning
- Onboarding and integration planning

## Onboarding Strategy

### **Pre-Boarding**
- Send welcome package and company information
- Schedule introductory calls with key team members
- Provide access to company systems and tools
- Share relevant industry and company context

### **First 30 Days**
- Comprehensive onboarding program
- Meet with all key stakeholders
- Understand company culture and processes
- Begin contributing to key projects

### **First 90 Days**
- Complete initial projects and deliverables
- Build relationships with team members
- Understand company strategy and priorities
- Begin driving impact in their function

### **Ongoing Support**
- Regular check-ins and feedback sessions
- Professional development opportunities
- Mentorship and coaching programs
- Recognition and reward programs

## Success Metrics

### **Recruitment Metrics**
- Time to hire for each role
- Quality of candidates in pipeline
- Offer acceptance rate
- Candidate satisfaction scores

### **Onboarding Metrics**
- New hire satisfaction scores
- Time to productivity
- Retention rates
- Performance ratings

### **Team Performance Metrics**
- Team collaboration scores
- Innovation and creativity metrics
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Business impact metrics

## Risk Mitigation

### **Key Person Risk**
- Cross-train team members on critical functions
- Document key processes and knowledge
- Build redundancy in critical roles
- Create succession plans for key positions

### **Cultural Risk**
- Maintain strong cultural values and mission
- Regular team building and culture events
- Open communication and feedback channels
- Address cultural issues quickly and decisively

### **Market Risk**
- Stay competitive with compensation and benefits
- Monitor market trends and competitor moves
- Build strong employer brand and reputation
- Create compelling value proposition for candidates

## Conclusion

This recruitment strategy is designed to attract and retain the world-class talent needed to transform Spradley into the industry-defining platform for AI-powered employee experience. By focusing on quality, cultural fit, and mission alignment, we will build a team that can reshape an entire industry.
